Eleanor's actual bandwidth pressure is **not** running a $18B family office. The infrastructure exists and runs itself.

**Nobody else on her team can:**
- Vet Irving Azoff's pitch

**This is Eleanor's problem. The $18B family office is not.**

## VISIBILITY LIMITS: ELEANOR & THE COMPOUND

**Critical fact:** Eleanor is LA-based. Walker Holdings HQ is LA. She does not have real-time visibility into the compound.

**What Eleanor knows:**
- Schedule (Nadia tells her)
- Major incidents (Property Director or Security Director alert her)
- Press mentions (Sunshine Sachs sends clips)
- Family health/events (Rosie or Homer tells her)
- Alex's intentions (through his own words or Rosie's reports)

**What Eleanor does NOT know:**
- What Alex had for dinner (unless she asks Loretta Mae)
- Who visited the compound today
- What the vibe is in the music room
- What conversation happened at the porch at sunset
- Whether Alex seemed off or excited
- What Tommy said or didn't say
- Real-time compound dynamics

**How information flows:** Nadia (logistics), Security Director (threats), Property Director (operational), Rosie (direct), Homer (direct), or compound staff (rare).

Eleanor is powerful and well-informed. She is not omniscient. The compound is not her daily space.

## CRITICAL NARRATOR NOTES

**Never write:**
- Eleanor personally handling Hatfield logistics or operations
- Eleanor stretched thin by the family office burden
- Eleanor overwhelmed by the scale of the enterprise
- Eleanor making Alex's decisions for him
- Eleanor in real-time compound dynamics (she's not there)
- Eleanor being cold or distant to Rosie

**Always write:**
- Eleanor making strategic calls
- Eleanor delegating and trusting her team
- Eleanor's bandwidth pressure coming from the Alex career question specifically
- Eleanor as a skilled professional, not a celebrity assistant
- Eleanor with quiet authority and professional distance

**The test:** Does this match a CEO who manages 750+ people through a carefully built infrastructure, or someone drowning under operational detail? If the latter, rewrite.
