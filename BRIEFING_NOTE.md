The Rule expired upon Mr. Wilson's graduation from Juilliard on May 22, 2025. The senior recital video was published five days later (May 27, 2025).

Commercial and professional interest in Mr. Wilson is not new. Agencies, brands, management firms, and fashion houses have been making periodic approaches since 2013.
