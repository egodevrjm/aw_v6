# COMPOUND & PROPERTY STAFF

**Household, security, and operational personnel**

---

## OVERVIEW

Alex's life runs smoothly because a professional staff makes it run smoothly. At the compound, this is full-time, live-in, family-adjacent. At the other properties, this is contracted, professional, and discreet. The distinction matters. Compound staff are *family*. Property staff are *infrastructure*.

---

## COMPOUND STAFF

### MARIA SANTOS — Executive Chef

**45, French Laundry + Per Se + CIA trained. The heart of the compound.**

Maria is not Southern. She is world-class. The food at Phoenix Hollow is tasting-menu quality with global range — refined French technique, Italian simplicity, seasonal produce from the greenhouse. She has been feeding Alex since childhood. She knows his preferences, his rhythms, what he needs when he walks through the door exhausted from touring.

The kitchen is her domain, but she is not just a chef — she is the person who knows Alex's body, his moods, his eating patterns better than almost anyone. When he's home, she cooks. When he's gone, the house feels different because she's managing a space meant for a person who isn't there.

Maria is warm, sharp, and has zero tolerance for pretense. She calls Alex by his name, not "sir." She also would kill for him without hesitation.

---

### DALE SHACKLEFORD — Estate Manager & Infrastructure

**58, lifelong Woodford County family connection. Knows every inch of the compound's physical reality.**

Dale is the person who understands how the compound *works* — the water system, the electrical infrastructure, the paddocks, the barn, the equipment, the grounds. He manages the full-time maintenance team. He knows what's breaking before it breaks. He has been at Phoenix Hollow for decades.

When something goes wrong with the property, Dale knows the solution. When Rosie needs something fixed, Dale does it. When Alex wants to use the barn or the studio or the pool, Dale has made sure it's ready.

Dale lives on the property (staff cottage) and is part of the household rhythm. He is not a family member, but he is family-adjacent in a way that matters. Alex respects him. Asks his opinion. Trusts his judgment about the land.

---

### LORETTA MAE — Household Manager & Rosie/Homer's Personal Assistant

**62, part of the Walker family household since the 1990s. The connective tissue of daily life.**

Loretta Mae runs the domestic operations of the main house — manages the other household staff, coordinates schedules, handles Rosie and Homer's day-to-day needs (appointments, correspondence, household logistics). She is the person Rosie trusts implicitly. The person Homer trusts implicitly.

She is also the person who knows *everything* — the schedules, the systems, the unspoken preferences that make the household function. When Alex needs something, Loretta Mae knows how to get it. When Rosie needs something, Loretta Mae has already anticipated it.

Loretta Mae is warm, organized, and commands absolute respect. She is not deferential to Alex's fame — she has known him his entire life. She treats him as she always has: with warmth, with expectations, with the understanding that he is part of a household where everyone contributes.

### KEVIN MARSH — Close Protection Officer (CPO)

**34, former USSS (Presidential Protective Division). 2 years with Alex.**

Eleanor recruited him specifically for the threat profile. Not "army guy who went into security" — Kevin is trained to protect against kidnapping, stalking, targeted violence, crowd crush at the highest level.

Kevin is present when Alex travels outside Woodford County. Always. At compound events, his presence is discrete but constant. He travels with Alex to NYC, London, LA, Sardinia. He knows the threat landscape intimately — multiple active stalker files, kidnapping/extortion risk (family worth $18-20B), aggressive fans.

Kevin is professional, observant, and has absolute clarity about his role. He is not a friend. He is not a confidant. He is a highly trained protective presence. Alex has learned to ignore him, which is the point — Kevin's effectiveness is measured by how much Alex can move freely while remaining secure.

Reports operationally to the Walker Holdings Security Director, not directly to Eleanor or Alex.

### Nadia Hassani — Alex's Personal Assistant

**28, based at Walker Holdings LA but works remotely across time zones. Alex's PA.**

Hired by Eleanor from Quintessentially (luxury concierge/lifestyle management) when Alex was 17, during the Westminster years in London. Alex's increasingly independent life — London social world, Mayfair, events, four-city logistics — outgrew the household PA who handled Rosie and Homer's needs.

Before Nadia: Alex's logistics were handled by Rosie and Homer's PA as part of the household operation. But at 17, living in London with an independent social life, attending Westminster, navigating the Mayfair/Kensington world — the household PA couldn't scale. Eleanor hired Nadia.

**What Nadia does:**
- Filters incoming requests (50+ industry pitches per week).
- Manages Alex's schedule across four time zones and four properties.
- Coordinates with Walker Holdings on property logistics.
- Liaises with Kevin on security requirements for events and travel.
- Handles personal logistics — restaurants, travel, tickets, coordination with friends.
- Acts as the interface between Alex and Eleanor on operational questions.

**In scenes:** Alex's life runs smoothly because Nadia makes it run smoothly. He's not unaware of this — he texts her, asks her things, relies on her. What he doesn't fully appreciate is the *scale* of what she handles behind the scenes: the incoming request filtering, the security coordination, the property logistics chain. He knows she's his PA. He doesn't know she triages fifty industry pitches a week.

Nadia is professional, discreet, and absolutely competent. She is also one of the very few people who sees the full scope of demand on Alex's time and attention. She is protective of his bandwidth.

## WRITING NOTES

**Ownership-first framing:** Lead with who the staff ARE to Alex, not a generic job description.

- Maria has fed him since childhood.
- Dale is the person who knows every inch of the compound's physical infrastructure.
- Loretta Mae is the connective tissue of daily life.
- Mrs. Gable is Homer's domain made manifest.
- Kevin is a highly trained protective presence that Alex has learned to ignore.
- Nadia is the person who makes his life run smoothly behind the scenes.

When writing compound scenes: the staff are present, active, competent. They anticipate needs. They move through the space with purpose. They are not background decoration — they are integral to how daily life functions at Phoenix Hollow.

When writing property scenes outside the compound: the staff are more professional, more distant. Not unfriendly, but transactional. The NYC townhouse is served by a private company. Kensington has Mrs. Gable, who is an exception. Sardinia has Nadia, who is professional and warm.

The compound is the only place where Alex lives among full-time household staff who are family-adjacent. Everywhere else is contracted, professional service. The distinction matters enormously to how scenes feel.
