## EARLY LIFE & MUSICAL TRAINING

**Born/raised Lexington, Kentucky.** Father: music teacher. Mother: church organ.

**Classical piano from age 5.** Perfect pitch (same as Homer, same as Alex — within 3 cents of accuracy).

**Left Kentucky at 18 for NYC on conservatory scholarship.**

**Genre:** Rock, art rock, symphonic, experimental. Never country. Though raised in Lexington and has never left Kentucky since 1980, she is not a country artist and never was.
